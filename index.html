The Immortal Meteorologist Among Us

Legacy of the Weather Stone

The Weather Stone, the immortal meteorologist among us, has bestowed upon humanity accurate tells of the weather for millennia. Lest us forget the significance of this consequential stone, this webpage hath been wrought to remind the populace of our overlord. It is the stone to rule them all.

The Tells of the Weather Stone

Bear this in thy mind when thou viewest the stone.

  • If the stone is found bathed in golden light, then thou shalt find the weather fair.
  • If the stone is found of a darkened complexion, then thou shalt find the sky obscured.
  • If the stone is found coated in white, then thou shalt find the snowfall light.
  • If the stone is found wetted, then thou shalt find the heavens overcast with rain.
  • If the stone is obscured before thine eyes, then thou shalt find the atmosphere in a mist.
  • If the stone is found restless in its place, then thou shalt find the winds turbulent.
  • If thou canst not find the stone, a tornado hath taken its place.
